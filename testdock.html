My Experience

After majoring in Fine Arts and Commercial Art in college, I began a long career with a concrete sculpture company, doing casting and finishing work. Although I eventually transitioned from the hands-on work into a leadership position, I came to fill an advisory role on the product design team and marketing.

More recently I have expanded my artistic range by studying UI/UX. After one year of coursework I am eager and ready to use my creative passion and design skills to help foster the easiest and clearest user experiences for your customers.
